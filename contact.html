Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a course?

Simply sign up for an account, browse our course catalog, and click "Enroll" on any course you're interested in.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. Payment plans are also available for select courses.

Can I get a refund?

Yes, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee if you're not satisfied with your course.

Do I get a certificate?

Yes, upon successful completion of a course, you'll receive a verified certificate of completion.
